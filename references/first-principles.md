# First-Principles Reconstruction

Before writing slides, reconstruct the material.

Include:

- the real topic, not just the source title
- the audience problem
- primitive concepts
- mechanism or causal chain
- contrasts and boundaries
- failure modes
- examples or analogies
- what the deck should emphasize
- what the deck should avoid

The output should make the deck deeper than a summary. It should explain why the topic works, where it breaks, and how the learner should reason about it.
